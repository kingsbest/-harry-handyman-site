02 — Pricing

Flat-rate & quotes

We work to flat-rate pricing. You get one clear price in pounds, agreed before we start — no hourly surprises and no creeping bills at the end.

No. Quotes are completely free and there's no obligation. Tell us about the job and we'll give you an honest price.

Call, WhatsApp or use the contact form and tell Harry about the job — sending photos over WhatsApp is the quickest way. You'll get one clear flat price back.

You can pay by bank transfer, cash or card, and payment is due on completion of the job. There's nothing to pay up front — you settle the agreed flat price once the work is finished and you're happy with it.

No — there's no call-out fee. You pay a flat rate agreed for the job, so what we quote is what you pay. For very small tasks it's often worth grouping a few together into one visit to get the best value.
